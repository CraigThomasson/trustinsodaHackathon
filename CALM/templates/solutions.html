Why is employee happiness good for business?

8 steps to accelerate workforce well-being and employee mindfulness

Based primarily on Kotter Inc.'s 8 Steps to Accelerate Change in Your Organization, the methods and principles below provide the framework for business leaders to adopt, build upon and implement actionable strategies geared towards their workforce's well-being and happiness. These 8-steps focus on the mindsets, attitudes, and behaviours needed to drive the desired change and transformation.

1. CREATE

a sense of urgency

Top leaders must describe an opportunity to accelerate their workforce's well-being that will appeal to individuals' heads + hearts, and use this statement to raise a large, urgent army of volunteers.

RESULTS: You'll have a breadth of mindfulness and staf well-being focused readiness across the organization.

Let's Talk

2. BUILD

a guiding coalition

The Guiding Coalition is, in many ways, the nerve center of the 8-Step Process. It can take many shapes, but must consist of members from multiple layers of the hierarchy, represent many functions, receive information about the organization at all levels and ranks, and synthesize that information into new ways of working.

Let's Talk

3. FORM

a strategic vision & initiatives

Dr. Kotter defines strategic initiatives as targeted and coordinated activities that, if designed and executed fast enough and well enough, will make your vision a reality. Characteristics of a terrific strategic vision:

  • Communicable
  • Desirable
  • Creates a Verbal Picture
  • Flexible
  • Feasible
  • Imaginable
  • Simple
Let's Talk


4. ENLIST

a volunteer army

Large-scale change can only occur when very significant numbers of employees amass under a common opportunity and drive in the same direction.

Accelerate explains that "history has demonstrated that it is possible to find many change agents … but only if people are given a choice, and feel they truly have permission to step forward and act." You must build excitement around the Big Opportunity and develop a feeling that one ”wants to" (vs. ”has to") contribute.

Let's Talk

5. ENABLE ACTION

by removing barriers

By removing barriers such as inefficient processes and archaic norms, leaders provide the freedom necessary for employees to work across boundaries and create real impact.

RESULTS: You'll have tangible evidence of employee motivation stemming from collapsed silos and new ways of working together.

Let's Talk
