8 steps to accelerate workforce well-being and employee mindfulness

Based primarily on Kotter Inc.'s 8 Steps to Accelerate Change in Your Organization, the methods and principles below provide the framework for business leaders to adopt, build upon and implement actionable strategies geared towards their workforce's well-being and happiness. These 8-steps focus on the mindsets, attitudes, and behaviours needed to drive the desired change and transformation.

1. CREATE

a sense of urgency

Top leaders must describe an opportunity to accelerate their workforce's well-being that will appeal to individuals' heads + hearts, and use this statement to raise a large, urgent army of volunteers.

Let's Talk

2. BUILD

a guiding coalition

Represented by managers, staff, senior executives across the company who also represent many job functions, the team will be able to receive input about the opportunities and challenges around their people's well-being and happiness at work.

Let's Talk

3. FORM

a strategic vision & initiatives

To target and coordinate activities that, if designed and executed fast enough and well enough, will make your vision to keep your employees happy, healthy and empowered.

Let's Talk

4. ENLIST

a volunteer army

Large-scale change can only occur when very significant numbers of employees amass under a common opportunity and drive in the same direction.

Let's Talk

5. ENABLE ACTION

by removing barriers

By removing barriers such as inefficient processes and archaic norms, leaders provide the freedom necessary for employees to work across boundaries and create real impact.

Let's Talk

6. GENERATE

short-term wins

Wins are the molecules of results. They must be collected, categorized and communicated (early and often) to track progress and energize your volunteers to drive change.

Let's Talk

7. SUSTAIN ACCELERATION

Press harder after the first success

So you have had a few wins. It can be easy to lift your foot off the gas pedal after experiencing some success. Instead, this is the time to press harder and use those wins as momentum to further fuel the change.

Let's Talk

8. INSTITUTE CHANGE

To ensure new behaviors are repeated over the long term, it's important that you define and communicate the connections between those behaviors and the organization's success.

Let's Talk
